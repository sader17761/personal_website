Pig Game Rules:

  • The 'Pig Game' consists of 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • During each turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as he/she wishes. The face value of each roll gets added to his/her CURRENT score.
  • After each roll, the player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his/her CURRENT score then gets added to his/her TOTAL score, and the roll of the dice goes to the other player.
  • If the player rolls a 1 with either die, all of his/her CURRENT score gets taken away, and the roll of the dice goes to the other player.
  • If the player rolls a double 1 (snake eyes), all of his/her TOTAL and CURRENT scores gets taken away (set back to 0), and the roll of the dice goes to the other player.
  • Play continues until the first player reaches 100 TOTAL points (the default score).
    • To play to a score other than 100, update the 'SET FINAL SCORE' input field at the bottom of the game board to which ever score you wish. This can be done at any point before or during the game.
