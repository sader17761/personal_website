Mille Lacs Lake

Mille Lacs Lake is where I've spent the least amount of time. I've only fished there once, this year for fishing opener. So to say my hot spot is the only one, I would be lying. This lake is 14 miles wide and 21 miles long, so I'm sure there are many more places to fish. My experience though, between two boats we caught 40 walleye in about 2.5 hours...not bad...and lots of fun. All of them caught using a bobber with a leach in about 12-16 feet of water.
