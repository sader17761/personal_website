O'Dowd Lake

O'Dowd Lake is one that is close to home, so it's one that I visit quite often. I typically have such good luck within a few hundred yards of the dock that I really haven't worked the entire lake (as you can see by my hot spots). On O'Dowd I'm usually fishing for largemouth bass (unless ice fishing), and I'll usually catch bass fishing with a wacky worm, jig, frog, crankbait, spinner, or rapala.
