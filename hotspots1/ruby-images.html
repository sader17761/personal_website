Ruby Lake

Ruby Lake is a lake that I'll never forget. I've only been there once, and that was for fishing opener back in 2012. My first time ever fishing for walleye. There was a group of us that went, and the deal was the first person to catch a fish, the largest fish, and the most fish would each win a little money. Well, it just so happened that I won the first, the biggest and the most. Not bad for a rookie. :)
My first and second fish were caught by using a linde rig with a leech, the others were caught using a bobber with a leech.
