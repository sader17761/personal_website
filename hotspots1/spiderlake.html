Spider Lake

Spider was another lake we hit during the 2012 fishing opener, although we didn't fish it opening day, we did fish it the day before for crappie (which is legal). Between the 6 of us fishing, we caught over 40 crappie... 20 or so we kept for a fish fry later that night. Most if not all were caught using minnows hooked to a jig.
