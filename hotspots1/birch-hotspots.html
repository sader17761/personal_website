Birch Lake

Birch Lake was a family vacation location in 2014. In each of my hot spot locations, we caught crappie, largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, and northern pike. We caught northern pike on just about anything we tossed out there, but the bass really liked top water frogs, and worms fished wacky style (lately my favorite way to fish).
