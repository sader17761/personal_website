Lake Chetac

Lake Chetac was also part of our 2014 family vacation. If you look closely at the map, Birch and Chetac Lakes are connected via a small channel on the south end (north end of Birch). The fishing was better on Birch (probably because it was smaller and easier to cover), but that said, we still caught a few bass here as well.
