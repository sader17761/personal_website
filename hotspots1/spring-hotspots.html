Spring Lake

Spring Lake is virtually in my back yard. I really haven't caught anything worthy of a fish tale, but it's always been a good lake to take the family out to catch some smaller bass and some crappie. In the winter on the other hand, it's been a great lake to catch a ton of crappie.
