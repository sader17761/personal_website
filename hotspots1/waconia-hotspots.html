Lake Waconia

Lake Waconia is one of my go to lakes when I'm not sure where I should go. The northern and western hot spots are virtually a guarantee when it comes to bass. I like to use a small spinner, or a shallow running rapala.
If you're looking for something a bit bigger, like a 40-50 inch muskie, I would hit up the sunken islands in the middle of the lake.
