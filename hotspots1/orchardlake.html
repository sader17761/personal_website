Orchard Lake

Orchard Lake is another one of my close to home favorites. With shallow and deep weed lines, there are plenty of places to find bass in this lake...because of the weeds, I tend to use more top water or shallow running spinners or rapalas. If bass aren't your thing, this is a good lake for northern pike and muskie.
