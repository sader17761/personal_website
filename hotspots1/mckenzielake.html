McKenzie Lake

McKenzie Lake is where we spent our family vacation in 2013. The northern and eastern locations were the most lucrative for largemouth bass. The northern end was best in the late afternoon into the evening using a wacky worm. The eastern side was best in the morning just over a week bed using top water lures (frogs, poppers, jerk baits and rapalas).
